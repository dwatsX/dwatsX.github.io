C# Programming Assignments

The image on the left displays a sample GUI laid out for a programming assignment, in which students were tasked with creating an airline reservation system. Although the sample GUI included buttons representing the seats, there was no requirement to make the buttons do anything, and served as a rather pointless visual representation.

What Dave Did

Finding the sample GUI to be stale and simplistic, Dave improved the design, and added dynamic functionality to the buttons. This allowed the buttons to visually indicate which seats were or were not reserved, according to their colour. It is this kind of innovative, outside the box thinking that makes Dave a valuable member of any team.
